History---------------------------------------------------------------------------

                Objective was to develop a business Software or ERP Software
    
    1980        company released 1st ERP Software by name SAP R/2
                    R : real time
                    2 : 2 tier architecture
    
    1990        company released advanced version of SAP R/2 by name SAP R/3
                    3 : 3 tier architecture
